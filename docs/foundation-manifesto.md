# Foundation Manifesto

Founder is real.
Belief is real.
Foundation is real.
Outcomes must be real.

Agent-Foundation exists because durable platforms cannot be built from fragments. They require a real origin, a coherent belief, shared language, evidence-backed contracts, and a community that extends the foundation without erasing it.

## We believe

We believe the founder is the first source of coherence.

We believe a foundation is not a document, a repository, or a legal form. A foundation is encoded belief made durable through principles, contracts, provenance, evaluation, and governance.

We believe platforms should not start from hype. They should start from reality.

We believe every claim should have evidence.

We believe every output should carry provenance.

We believe trust must be earned through evaluation.

We believe community should extend coherence, not fragment it.

We believe partners should amplify capability, not replace accountability.

We believe products should deliver real outcomes, not imaginary value.

It is the foundation.
